When my personal site on Netlify, I used to have a contact form using their serverless form handling, but it was finnicky and ultimately something I don't need for a platform that is in essence a logbook for myself.

Even though I appreciate forming part of communities (specially other fellow indie webmasters), I won't provide a way to directly contact me through this site.

If you still need to contact me in any way, you may do so through one of these social media accounts.

  • Mastodon @indiepocalypse.
  • Bluesky @kulugary.itch.io.
  • Tumblr @kulugary.

If you want to contact me about a blog post, you may send me a webmention about it and it will show up in it whenever the site is rebuilt (right now, once a week).
